Biography

I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the State University of New York at Buffalo (UB), under the direction of Prof. Wenyao Xu at the Embedded Sensing and Computing (ESC) Lab. I received my Bachelor Degree in Computer Engineering from UB during May 2017.

My research interests focus on IoT, Cyber-Physical Security & Privacy and Smart Health.
